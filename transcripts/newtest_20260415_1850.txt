Ekhla chavdzaro takhali shekhodra. Shekhodra zvart, meda salomen. Shorodzhova, mena salomen. Tghez kanuhilot jekichaniz mumavali. Davet kuatrom va birvali nakhevari tseli minimum ikneba tselen stresuli periodi. Vas pikrob magaza? Stresuli periodi ari, mteve taso alagepeli ari. Kadrebi, stafinko, taso matabeli. Hariski ari, taso standartabeli kolopili yongshiro verti ta igiwe kemo kadio dosi. Am kolopesro da alagep, tmere gatavoti, vartuka shemda getavze. Tser chavkhurot birvali etapi. Shekhodra dasrula bulia.
